"I think I am getting the hang of this." says Veronica. Her stance steady, she lines up her other hand with the target, she throws the axe. THUNK. "Yes! right on the 30 point marker". "Nice job let me take a crack" says Cameron. He grabs an axe and without much wait chucks it. "YEET" The axe handle hits the outermost ring and it falls to the ground. "You are focusing too much on power Cam" Rails says. "You aren't much better"  Cameron retorts. Rails walks up to the throwing line. 

"So I recently had a very strange dream" Rails says. They grab an axe. "In it I was the Winnipeg Meteor" Rails takes a a second to stop and line up their stance. "And when I landed this nurse is staring me down, looking into my soul" Sleet brings their arm back for a throw. "I made eye contact but I still felt guilty" Sleet throws the axe. It lands almost in the bullseye. "And then you caught fire? I had this same dream last month" Cameron interrupts. "You caught fire? In mine a feather came out of my mouth" Veronica says. Rail collects their axe. "Shut up. I started to sweat ballistic jelly and then I woke up. And apparently everyone I know had this dream, so how did your's go" Rails says, gesturing at Dina and Helen. "I haven't had this dream" says Helen. "I had it, nothing after the spectators thou, I just woke up" Dina responds. "Well what ever it means, I hope fire is good. Let me take another shot, I'd like to get one axe in the board before we leave for dinner." Cameron says.

"One last throw!" Cameron cries. "You hit the target already, twice. We are going to dinner now, with or without you." Dina yells back.  "Ok fine." He responds.  The group files out on to the street. Dina and Rail argue, Helen and Crownell flirt, and Helen buts in every so often. Veronica smiles. Then they see it. A flash in the sky. Everyone grows quiet.

"Is that coming towards us?" Rail says. Their breath escapes them a little. "I think so" Cameron replies. It is getting closer. The eerie similarity to the dream does not evade Rail. It likely doesn't evade many of their friends either. A warm wind rushes by the group. "Should we do s-" says Cameron.

Then everything goes dark for Rail.
