Rail is sitting on top of a heating unit. It hums and rattles below them. Rail isn't often early but Veronica would secretly be sad if they were late. The spring breeze flows dragging up junk from under the curb. The sound of chatter and footsteps in the distance grows louder. That's probably Crownel along with a few of other friends of theirs. As the get in eavesdropping distance Rail can tell its Veronica, Crownel, and Crownel's girlfriend who's name currently evades Rail's memory.
"Hey Crow!" Rail yells. Crownel's head turns sharply in Rail's direction. "How the fuck did you get up there?" They respond. "Does it really matter? I am here early or on time. Which probably wont be the case for the rest of our friends." Rail carefully gets down from the unit and offers Veronica a hug. She accepts. "Flesh said they can't make it and you know how Cameron is. So that only leaves Diana and Daniel" replies Veronica. Cameron hates meeting anywhere but the final location. He has demonstrated this almost every time they have had to meet up for the last year. Rail knows that Veronica is worried about Flesh, but now is not the time to get hung up on Veronica or Flesh's mental health issues.
"Are they going to make us wait here for a whole hour?" says Veronica. The irritation stretches out her complaint. "I could message them" replies Crownell. "No Need! Daniel can't make it but I'm here." Dina calls though her panting. 

"Let's head over then" replies Veronica.
